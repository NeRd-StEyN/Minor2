# Government Welfare Schemes - Sample (2025)

## 1. PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi
*   **Eligibility:** All land-holding farmer families.
*   **Benefits:** ₹6,000 per year paid in three equal installments.
*   **Documentation:** Aadhaar card, Bank Account details, Land ownership papers.

## 2. Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)
*   **Purpose:** Health insurance cover for secondary and tertiary care.
*   **Benefits:** Up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.
*   **Eligibility:** Determined by SECC 2011 data (for poor and vulnerable families).

## 3. Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)
*   **Goal:** Housing for all in rural and urban areas.
*   **Financial Assistance:** Up to ₹1.2 lakh (plain areas) and ₹1.3 lakh (hilly/difficult areas) for house construction.
